About Us

Our school founded in 2006, the school is a comprehensive modern business and it college, located in the center of Sydney Central Business District, Australia.

We offer a series of nationally recognized professional qualifications in the fields of software development (it), marketing management, project management, business and leadership training. The content and structure of our courses are regularly reviewed by our academic committee and industry consultants to ensure that the courses meet the rapidly changing needs of the market. The quality training we provide at WIA will certainly help you gain solid professional knowledge in your field of study and incorporate the assurance approach (with high reputation) into the degree courses offered by Australian universities.
